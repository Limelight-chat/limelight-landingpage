## The AI Memory System for Your Business

Your company’s knowledge is trapped. Sales data is locked in spreadsheets, financial reports are buried in PDFs, and vital operational context is scattered across Slack, WhatsApp, and emails. Individually, these tools work fine. But together, they create a massive bottleneck: your company’s intelligence is fundamentally disconnected.

When executives or operators ask critical questions—like "Why did revenue drop last quarter?" or "Which suppliers caused the most delivery delays?"—finding the answer requires digging through files, cleaning messy spreadsheets, combining sources, and waiting on analysts to build queries. What should take seconds often takes days.

Limelight was built to fix this.

### The Problem: Disconnected and Messy Data

Most companies don’t suffer from a lack of data; they suffer from too much disconnected, unstructured data. To make matters worse, real-world data is rarely clean. It is full of inconsistent formats, duplicate records, and hidden tables embedded inside documents. Before analysis can even begin, analysts typically spend 60–80% of their time just preparing the data.

### Limelight: The Intelligence Layer for Your Business

Limelight is an AI memory system for your company. It securely indexes your siloed data—across structured databases and unstructured documents—and unifies it into a single intelligence layer.

### What Limelight Actually Does

Once connected, Limelight automates the heavy lifting of data preparation and analysis:

1. **Ingests Data From Anywhere**: Limelight natively understands PDFs, spreadsheets, databases, internal tools, and messaging platforms, creating a unified knowledge graph across your organization.

2. **Cleans and Understands Your Data**: Real-world data is messy. Limelight automatically detects anomalies like inconsistent column values, hidden document tables, and formatting issues. It interprets and organizes this information so it is instantly ready for analysis.

3. **Turns Natural Language Into Analysis**: Instead of the traditional workflow—opening a BI tool, writing a query, building a calculation, and validating the result—you just ask. Ask, “Which sales region generated the most profit relative to team size?” and Limelight analyzes the relevant data to return an instant result.

4. **Searches Across All Company Knowledge**: Acting like a private search engine for your business, Limelight lets you instantly retrieve reports, support logs, and operational data without needing to know exactly where the file lives.

5. **Bridges the Gap Between Analysts and Decision Makers**: This system doesn’t replace analysts—it amplifies them. By allowing anyone in the organization to ask complex data questions and get immediate answers, analysts are freed from answering repetitive queries and can focus on deeper, strategic work.

### Why Traditional BI Tools Aren’t Enough

Business intelligence tools were designed for structured dashboards. But modern companies deal with far more than structured tables. Crucial insights often live in documents, support logs, and unstructured text—areas where traditional BI tools struggle.

Limelight is designed from the ground up to reason over both structured and unstructured data, answering the nuanced questions that standard dashboards simply cannot.
