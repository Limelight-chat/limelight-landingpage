# Integrations

Limelight connects with the tools you use every day to bring all your data into one searchable place.

## Supported Integrations

### Google Drive

Connect your Google Drive to search across Docs, Sheets, Slides, and PDFs.

### Slack

Index your Slack conversations to find answers hidden in threads and channels.

### Notion

Search your Notion workspace, including pages, databases, and comments.

## Connecting a New Integration

1.  Go to **Settings > Integrations**.
2.  Click **Connect** next to the service you want to add.
3.  Follow the authorization prompts.
